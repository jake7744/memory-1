# 🎨 Matrixonic Business Card Kit

- **브랜드 시스템**
  - Primary Palette: `Deep Navy Blue (#0A1932)`, `Muted Slate (#2C3E50)`, `Orange Accent (#F47B28)`
  - Typography Scale:
    - Heading: `600`, `18px`
    - Body: `400`, `14px`
    - Caption: `400`, `12px`
  - Radius: `8px` for cards, `24px` corners on interactive surfaces
- **3D 카드 컴포넌트**
  - Front Side:
    - Company wordmark in deep navy with subtle glow or prism highlight.
    - Key info stacked vertically with optical alignment.
  - Back Side:
    - Social links in orange accent.
    - Minimal state cues for hover/pressed/focus-visible.
- **가이드라인**
  - Use real-world depth hierarchy, not just shadows.
  - Keep content dense but readable on dark surfaces.
  - Ensure CTA contrast at least `4.5:1` against navy background.
